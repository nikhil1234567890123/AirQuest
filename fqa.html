Frequently Asked Questions

To book a flight, visit our website and enter your departure and arrival destinations, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Select your preferred flight options from the available choices, enter passenger details, and proceed to payment to confirm your booking.

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and online payment platforms such as PayPal. The available options will be displayed during the booking process.

Yes, depending on the fare conditions of your ticket, you may be able to make changes or cancel your flight. Visit the "Manage Booking" section on our website, enter your reservation details, and follow the instructions to make the desired changes. Please note that certain fares may have restrictions or fees for changes or cancellations.

The baggage allowance depends on your ticket type, destination, and the airline's policy. Generally, both checked baggage and carry-on baggage have specific weight and size restrictions. We recommend checking the baggage allowance section on our website or contacting our customer service for detailed information.

You can check-in for your flight through our website or mobile app. Enter your reservation details, select the check-in option, and follow the prompts to complete the process. Alternatively, you can also check-in at the airport counters if you prefer.

The required travel documents vary based on your destination and citizenship. Generally, you will need a valid passport for international travel. Some countries may require additional documents such as visas or proof of vaccinations. It is crucial to review the entry requirements for your destination country and ensure you have all the necessary documents before traveling.

Yes, we provide special assistance for passengers with disabilities, medical conditions, or specific needs. During the booking process or before your flight, you can inform us about any special requirements you have, such as wheelchair assistance, dietary restrictions, or medical equipment. Our customer service team will assist you accordingly.

In the event of a flight delay or cancellation, we will make every effort to notify you in advance through the contact information provided during booking. We will assist you with rebooking on the next available flight or provide alternate options as per our policy. It's recommended to regularly check your flight status on our website or contact our customer service for the latest updates.
